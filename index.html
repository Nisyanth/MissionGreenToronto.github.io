Blog

Year-round, our team of writers publishes interesting articles about the environment and how we can keep it clean.

Clean-ups

We have also done cleanups in places of need like Thomson Park and the Agincourt neighbourhood.

The team hosts fundraisers such as bake sales and shoe drives to help raise money for larger international organizations like the WWF.
