Our Events

Mission Green Toronto

Clean up

Mission Green Toronto has conducted a post-halloween clean up near Military Trail. We had a great turn out, with many people from the community showing up to help.







Bake/goods sale

On November 25th, we were at Chester Village's Christmas Bazaar to help spread the holiday spirit and our message about preserving the environment. We had a great time meeting new people and raised $213.45 towards our fundraising campaign for next year's CN Tower Climb for Nature.




Charity soccer match

Mission Green Toronto hosted a charity soccer match at the scarborough soccer complex. We had a great time watching all the games, and in total raised $447.50 to support the WWF-Canada!



Workshop

Mission Green Toronto held a workshop at Eglington Square Library to teach kids how to properly dispose waste, and how to perserve our environment.
